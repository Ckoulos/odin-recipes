Yummy Boi Rice

Back

An aromatic rice dish with a kick!

Sure to make your tastebuds say "Oh boi!"

Indredients

  • 1 1/2 C Wild Rice
  • 2 C Chicken Stock
  • 8 Baby Carrots, Diced
  • 1 Yellow Onion, Diced
  • 3 Cloves Garlic, Crushed
  • 1 Bell Pepper, Diced
  • 2 Sticks Celery, Diced
  • 4 Tbsp Butter
  • 4 tsp Cajun Seasoning

Steps

  1. In rice cooker pot, brown carrots, onion, celery, garlic, and bell pepper in butter.
  2. Once vegetables soften and gain color, add wild rice to pot.
  3. Add chicken stock to pot.
  4. Add cajun seasoning to pot. Stir well.
  5. Cook on 'Brown Rice' setting for full cycle
  6. Fluff rice and serve.

Serves 4
