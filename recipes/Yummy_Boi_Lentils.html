Yummy Boi Lentils

Back

An savory legume dish of your dreams!

Will satisfy all your guests, regardless of dietary restrictions.

Indredients

  • 4 Tbsp Butter
  • 12 Baby Carrots, Diced
  • 1/2 Sweet Onion, Diced
  • 3 Cloves Garlic, Crushed
  • 2 C Green Lentils
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 2 tsp Fennel Seeds, Crushed
  • Fresh Basil, Chopped

Steps

  1. Boil water, or broth.
  2. Add ingredients to water.
  3. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Drain as needed once timer is completed.
  5. Serve with chopped basil as garnish.

Serves 4
